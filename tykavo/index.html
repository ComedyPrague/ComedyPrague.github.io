About Us

Looking for a real local comedy experience in Prague? Join us at stand-up comedy open mic at Ty Kávo!, a relaxed Wednesday-night show where comedians come to test new jokes and audiences get an unfiltered, authentic comedy experience.

The atmosphere is informal and friendly — perfect whether you’re a comedy fan, a curious traveler, or just looking for a fun night out with good drinks and laughter.

Get a Spot

Feeling brave? Sign up with the MC for 5 minutes on stage.

It's best to do it in advance via the contact form below, but you can also just come to the show and sign on the spot.
